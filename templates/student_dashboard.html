1

Wait for a Session

Your teacher starts an attendance session for your class. It will appear on the left.

Click "Get Location & Generate QR". Your browser will ask for GPS permission — allow it.

3

4

Verification

System checks your GPS matches the classroom (within 30 m) and session is still active.
